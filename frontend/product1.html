Our Pigeon Varieties

King Pigeon ▼

The King Pigeon is a large breed known for its broad body and calm temperament, often bred for exhibition.

Fantail Pigeon ▼

Has a beautiful fan-shaped tail with up to 40 feathers. Often used in ornamental shows and exhibitions.

Jacobins ▼

Distinguished by a feathered hood covering the head and neck. They look very royal and elegant.

Modena Pigeonr ▼

Compact and colorful, available in many patterns. Originated in Italy and popular for exhibitions.

Capuchine Pigeon ▼

Smaller version of Jacobins with a lighter feather hood. Known for its graceful and unique appearance.

Lahore Pigeon▼

Very attractive with white wings and a colored body. Popular as a pet and show pigeon due to its beauty.

Carrier Pigeon▼

Once widely used for sending messages in wars and old times. Has a long beak and upright posture.

Frillback Pigeon▼

Special for its curly or frilled feathers on wings and body. Mostly bred for beauty and display.

Homing Pigeon▼

Famous for its strong homing instinct, can return to its loft from hundreds of kilometers away. Mostly used in pigeon racing and message carrying.

Racing Homer▼

Racing Homers are bred for speed and endurance, used in pigeon racing competitions worldwide.
